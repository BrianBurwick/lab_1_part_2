Alien Life From Neighboring Galaxy Makes Contact With Earth

What Will Happen Next?

NASA announced early Monday morning that over the past weekend the source of the "Hum of the Universe" was discovered to be a neighboring alien civilization from a nearby galaxy. Scientists are currently unable to translate the contents of the message and a global effort is currently underway with language specialists and artifical intelligence in order to attempt to understand them. The President of the United States is set to hold a press briefing on Tuesday morning at 9:00am with the head of NASA to answer questions and provide additional information.
